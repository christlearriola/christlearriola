# Case Study: M&A Technology Integration, Platform Consolidation & Day-1 Readiness

## 📌 Executive Summary
*   **The Challenge:** Orchestrating the post-merger technology integration of acquired global entities (including a major $12.7B acquisition) with highly fragmented legacy financial, expense, and ERP infrastructures. The primary objective was securing absolute "Day-1" operational readiness across cross-functional global systems without a single second of platform downtime.
*   **The Solution:** Structured and executed a centralized Integration Management Office (IMO) playbook. This framework automated the migration from legacy spreadsheets/ERP systems into a unified ERP and global travel & expense (SAP/Concur) platform, standardizing financial ledgers, secure SWIFT payment pathways, and automated FX hedging workflows.
*   **The Impact:** Achieved **100% Day-1 operational readiness** across dual Agile and Waterfall ecosystems, successfully migrated **10,000+ active users** to standard consolidated networks, and reduced transaction banking analysis lag from 5 days to real-time.

## 🛠️ Key Implementation Pillars

<details>
<summary><b>1. Centralized IMO Governance & Day-1 Workstreams</b></summary>

To manage the massive scale of a $12.7B technical integration, we split execution into highly specialized workstreams with rigid daily checkpoint tracking:
*   Established cross-functional standups spanning corporate treasury, legal, IT infrastructure, and product engineering.
*   Designed a critical-path dependency map tracking over 500+ operational items, ensuring that security permissions, identity access management (IAM), and key software licenses were active on Day-1.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>2. Core Financial Platform & Treasury Convergence</b></summary>

Replacing fragmented ledger operations with centralized enterprise pathways:
*   **ERP Consolidation:** Engineered the extraction and translation of historical financial data from inherited legacy databases, loading them cleanly into a consolidated ERP configuration with zero ledger discrepancies.
*   **SWIFT & FX Automation:** Directed the secure integration of automated SWIFT payment pathways and multi-bank API gateways, consolidating global exposure analyses from 5 days to real-time across a $200M+ distribution network.
*   **eBAM Implementation:** Protected newly acquired portfolios from compliance lapses by constructing automated electronic Bank Account Management (eBAM) workflows, shortening signatory update turnaround times by 50%.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>3. User Migration & Tool Consolidation (SAP/Concur)</b></summary>

Consolidating user applications across disparate business divisions:
*   Transitioned over 10,000 active global users from isolated, manual expense tracking platforms to a unified, standard SAP/Concur ecosystem.
*   Created automated API data sync mapping layers that decreased manual entry processing lag and eliminated up to 60% of localized transaction errors.
</details>
